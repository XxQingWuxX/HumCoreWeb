How Seth and Horus’s Mythological Conflict Shaped the Politics of Ancient Egypt

Working Title

How can you phrase the main topic of your research project in an engaging or intriguing way?

In order to phrase the main topic of my research paper in an intriguing way, I’ve realized just incorporating topics such as politics of Ancient Egypt or gods being worshiped during that era itself would be quite boring, but combining the two together using the dramas that occur in their stories located in pyramids would be something that might lure viewers interest.

A concise introduction to the chosen topic

To invite others to see what you are seeing, how concisely can you explain the primary source(s) you will be analyzing and the topic or aspect on which your analysis will focus?

To invite viewers into seeing what I’m seeing, the paper will be in order, divided into sections. Each section with lead into the next so the paper would be consistent, easy for viewers to follow. Not just that, including pictures from the primary sources would also give viewers senses of my idea. If unfortunately they couldn’t see what I’m seeing, then of course I’ll have tons of areas that need improvement, but so far I think the order of my paper should be ok.

Your humanistic research questions and method(s)

Since in Ancient Egypt, the gods themselves are representing nature by using titles of queens and texts from pyramids, there’s evidence provided to see the lands that represent the gods and its resources flowing into each other.
